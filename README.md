What are the real-world consequences of exposing an API key on GitHub?
Exposing a key can lead to "account takeover," where malicious actors use your quota, resulting in massive financial charges or the service being disabled. In a healthcare setting, it could also provide an entry point for attackers to intercept sensitive data streams.

Why does your company's privacy policy prohibit logging city names?
Logging city names violates the principle of Data Minimization. Under laws like HIPAA or GDPR, location data is sensitive; if a log file is breached, it could help identify specific patients and their movements, compromising their right to privacy.
